Dublin-pubs-app

A view of all the pubs that me and Kev have drank in, in Dublin

View project on GitHub

Idea Behind Dublin Pubs

According to the revenue there are some 800 licensed public houses in County Dublin. My Brother and I have decided that we shall drink in them all. These apps are just a small side project to try and keep track of the drinking.

Possible Re-Use

No point in creating a public page for the apps as they are fairly specific (and basic) but feel free to copy anything that you may find useful. I'm trying to keep all the components separate, which hopefully means that each lib is fairly self explanatory. Also, the hosting of the app at the moment is on openshift. They offer free gears for test apps so the backend is running on WildFly and the datastore is PostgreSQL.
